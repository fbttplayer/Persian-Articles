# Persian Articles

A collection of Persian-language essays by **Farsin Banki** (فرزین بانکی).

## About the Author

Farsin Banki is a Swiss-Iranian philosopher of education and cultural mediator between the German-speaking world and Iran. He holds a doctorate in the history of pedagogy and has taught at the University of Zurich. His work moves between Western philosophy, Iranian intellectual traditions, and the practice of intercultural dialogue.
